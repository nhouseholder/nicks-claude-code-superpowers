# Audience Monetization Strategy

Turn followers into paying customers. Review the business model, build the offer stack, design the funnel, and map the content angles that move people from follower to buyer.

- "Turn followers into customers"
- "Build my offer stack"

### Phase 3: Funnel Architecture

Map the follower-to-buyer journey:

```
[Social Content]
     ↓ (hook + value)
[Follow / Subscribe]
     ↓ (consistent value over 2-4 weeks)
[Lead Magnet Offer]
     ↓ (email sequence / DM sequence)
[Low-Ticket Purchase]
     ↓ (deliver value, build trust)
[Core Offer Pitch]
     ↓ (case studies, results, urgency)
[Mid/High-Ticket Purchase]
     ↓ (exceptional experience)
[Referral / Advocacy]
```

For each transition, define:
- **Trigger:** What prompts the person to move to the next stage
- **Content type:** What content supports this transition
- **Timeline:** How long this stage typically takes
- **Conversion rate benchmark:** What % is realistic

## Rules

1. **Revenue projections must be conservative** — better to under-promise. Flag all numbers as estimates.
2. **Offer must match audience** — don't suggest high-ticket if audience can't afford it
3. **Funnel must be buildable** — one person, realistic timeline
4. **No MLM/hustle-bro tactics** — no "passive income" promises, no fake scarcity
5. **Anti-slop** — no "multiple income streams" or "monetize your passion" filler
6. **Cross-reference upstream files** — offers must align with positioning, content must align with pillars
7. **Price anchoring must be honest** — compare to real alternatives, not inflated straw men
