# Content Pillars That Convert

Build 5 content pillars for a brand that consistently attract new followers, establish credibility, and drive leads or sales.

## When This Fires

- "Build my content pillars"
- "What should I post about?"
- "Content pillar strategy"
- "Define my content themes"
- Before building a content calendar (pillars should exist first)

## Prerequisites

1. **Read `brand-context.md`** — Required.
2. **Read `audience-profile.md`** if it exists — pillars must address audience psychology.
3. **Read `authority-positioning.md`** if it exists — pillars must reinforce positioning.
4. **Read `social-strategy.md`** if it exists — pillars must fit the overall strategy.

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Pillar Selection

Select 5 pillars that balance the content funnel:

| Pillar Role | Purpose | Funnel Stage |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| **Pillar 1: Expertise** | Demonstrate deep knowledge in your niche | Trust |
| **Pillar 2: Results/Proof** | Show outcomes, case studies, data | Conversion |
| **Pillar 3: Process/Behind-the-Scenes** | Show how you work, build transparency | Interest |
| **Pillar 4: Industry Commentary** | Hot takes, trends, analysis | Reach/Awareness |
| **Pillar 5: Personal/Relatable** | Human side, journey, lessons learned | Connection |

Customize pillar names and focus areas to the specific brand. These are starting templates, not final.

### Phase 2: Deep Pillar Definition

For each pillar, provide:

```
PILLAR [N]: [Name]
───────────────────
PURPOSE: [What this pillar does for your brand]
AUDIENCE CONNECTION: [Why your specific audience cares about this]
CONTENT RATIO: [What % of total content this should be]

EXAMPLE TOPICS (10 each):
1. [Specific topic with headline]
2. [Specific topic with headline]
...10. [Specific topic with headline]

PLATFORM FORMATS:
- Twitter/X: [Best format — thread, single tweet, poll, etc.]
- Instagram: [Best format — carousel, reel, story, etc.]
- YouTube: [Best format — long-form, short, community post]
- TikTok: [Best format — trend, tutorial, talking head, etc.]
- LinkedIn: [Best format — article, post, document, etc.]

HOOKS THAT WORK FOR THIS PILLAR:
- "[Hook template 1]"
- "[Hook template 2]"
- "[Hook template 3]"

CONTENT-TO-CONVERSION BRIDGE:
[How posts in this pillar lead toward monetization]
```

### Phase 3: Pillar Interaction Map

Show how pillars feed each other:

```
[Industry Commentary] → catches attention
         ↓
[Expertise] → proves you know your stuff
         ↓
[Results/Proof] → shows it actually works
         ↓
[Process] → reveals the "how" (creates desire)
         ↓
[Personal] → builds trust for the purchase decision
```

Customize the flow to the specific brand.

### Phase 4: Weekly Content Mix

Recommend a weekly posting schedule using pillar ratios:

| Day | Platform | Pillar | Format | Goal |
|-----|----------|--------|--------|------|
| Mon | Twitter | Expertise | Thread | Trust |
| Tue | Instagram | Results | Carousel | Proof |
| Wed | Twitter | Commentary | Hot take | Reach |
| Thu | YouTube | Process | Tutorial | Interest |
| Fri | Twitter | Personal | Story tweet | Connection |
| Sat | Instagram | Expertise | Reel | Trust |
| Sun | Twitter | Commentary | Thread | Reach |

Adjust frequency to what the user can sustain.

### Phase 5: Evergreen vs. Timely

For each pillar, identify:
- **Evergreen topics** (5 each) — can be posted anytime, repurposed, recycled
- **Timely triggers** — events/news that activate this pillar (e.g., "New season starts → Results pillar activates")

### Phase 6: Stage-Aware Lead Pillar

Based on the brand's current growth stage, recommend which pillar to lead with THIS month:

| Stage | Lead Pillar | Why | Ratio Shift |
|-------|------------|-----|------------|
| **NEW** (0-500 followers) | Awareness/Commentary | Need eyeballs first | 40% awareness, 25% expertise, 20% personal, 10% proof, 5% conversion |
| **GROWING** (500-5K) | Expertise/Authority | Converting attention to trust | 30% expertise, 25% awareness, 20% proof, 15% personal, 10% conversion |
| **MONETIZING** (5K+) | Results/Conversion | Trust exists, time to convert | 25% conversion, 25% proof, 20% expertise, 20% awareness, 10% personal |

### Phase 7: Repurposing Engine

Show how ONE piece of content becomes posts across all pillars:

```
ORIGINAL: [One deep-dive piece of content]
         ↓
AWARENESS: [Extract the hot take / contrarian angle]
TRUST:     [Pull the process / how-I-did-it angle]
AUTHORITY: [Highlight the data / results angle]
CONVERSION:[Frame as proof that your offer works]
```

Provide a concrete example using one topic from each pillar.

## Output

Save to `content-pillars.md` in the project root. Structure:
1. Pillar overview table (4-5 pillars at a glance, mapped to funnel stages)
2. Deep pillar definitions (all pillars)
3. Pillar interaction map
4. Weekly content mix schedule
5. Stage-aware lead pillar recommendation
6. Repurposing engine with example
7. Evergreen topic bank (25+ topics total)
8. Timely trigger calendar

## Rules

1. **5 pillars max** — more than 5 dilutes focus. Exactly 5.
2. **50 example topics minimum** — 10 per pillar. Specific, not vague.
3. **Platform-specific formats** — carousel advice for Twitter is useless. Match format to platform.
4. **Conversion path** — every pillar must connect to revenue, even indirectly
5. **Anti-slop** — no "valuable content" or "engage your audience" filler. Specific topics, specific hooks.
6. **Brand-aligned** — every pillar must trace back to something in brand-context.md
